Rupee Collector

You will be given a random number to start the game.

There are four Rupees below. By clicking on a Rupee, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Rupee is hidden until you click on it.

Be careful! The value of each Rupee will change at the start of each new game.
